About Me

Hello friends! My name is Nihila Arivalahan and I'm from Trichy. I am pursuing my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering.Writing was once my hobby but now it's my passion.
I've written one solo book and four anthologies. Among the four anthologies two got published and remaining two anthologies will be published soon. I strongly believe that, following our passion
will definitely give us satisfaction and happiness. Because I haven't dreamt of becoming a writer but now I'm the author of one book and coauthor of four other books. Apart from this, I love cooking,
dancing, listening to music and playing shuttle cock. Having many hobbies is a better way to be relaxed. My dream is to make people optimistic and I wish them to be a fervid and tenacious person too.
